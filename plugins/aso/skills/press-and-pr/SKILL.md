## Methodology

**Press & PR** — Get coverage that drives installs, backlinks, and App Store credibility.

### Why Press Matters for ASO

- Web Referral tracking in App Store Connect — measure press-driven downloads directly
- High-authority backlinks improve App Store search ranking signals
- "As seen in TechCrunch" on screenshots improves conversion rate
- Editorial featuring consideration increases with media visibility
- Organic sharing amplification after articles go live

### Target Media Tiers

**Tier 1 (high impact, harder to get):**
- TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired, Fast Company
- 9to5Mac, MacStories (iOS-specific, highly credible)
- Bloomberg, WSJ (for breakthrough apps)

**Tier 2 (accessible, still meaningful):**
- AppAdvice, TouchArcade (for games), AppShopper
- Niche newsletters in your category (health tech, productivity, fintech, etc.)
- YouTube channels with 50K+ subscribers in your category
- Podcasts with your target audience

**Tier 3 (start here, build momentum):**
- Reddit (r/iphone, r/androidapps, category-specific subreddits)
- Product Hunt
- Hacker News "Show HN"
- Indie Hackers
- Twitter/X community

### Story Angles That Get Coverage

| Angle | Example | Works For |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Numbers | "1M users in 30 days" | Any viral growth story |
| Problem solved | "How we fixed sleep for insomniacs" | Health, wellness |
| Unique data | "Our users are 47% more productive" | B2B, productivity |
| Category creation | "The first app for X" | Novel concepts |
| David vs. Goliath | "Solo developer beating $100M competitor" | Indie apps |
| Timely | App solving a problem in the news | Any category |
| Human story | Founder's personal connection to the problem | Any category |

### Press Kit Contents

Host at `yourwebsite.com/press`. Include:
- App name and one-liner (< 15 words)
- 3 founder photos (hi-res, 2MB+, professional)
- App icon (1024×1024px PNG)
- 5–10 screenshots (phone mockups, transparent or white background)
- Preview video (MP4, 720p+)
- Founder bio (3 sentences)
- Key stats (users, ratings, downloads if shareable)
- Prior media coverage links (if any)
- App Store link
- Press contact email

### Cold Pitch Email

**Structure (5 sentences max):**

1. Personalized opener: "I loved your piece on [specific article] — this might interest your readers."
2. News hook: "We just launched [app], which [does X for Y audience]."
3. Why it's a story: "[Angle — data point, problem solved, unique approach]."
4. Proof: "[Rating, user count, or notable achievement]."
5. Ask: "Happy to send a TestFlight link and press kit if you'd like a look: [press kit URL]"

**Never attach files** — link to your press kit URL.

**Send timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10am in the journalist's time zone.

**Follow-up:** Once only, after 5 business days. If no reply, move on.

### Finding Journalists

1. Search the target publication for recent articles in your category
2. Note the author's byline
3. Check their Twitter/X for contact preference
4. Use Hunter.io to find their email format
5. Verify the format with email validation

### Embargo Strategy

For major launches:
1. Contact 1–2 journalists per tier 2 weeks before launch
2. Offer exclusive (one outlet per tier — "I'm offering TechCrunch exclusive among tier-1 publications")
3. Provide TestFlight access immediately
4. Set embargo date = launch day

Embargoes build anticipation and guarantee coverage on launch day.

### Product Hunt Launch

**2 weeks before:** Create Coming Soon page, start collecting followers.

**1 week before:** Find a hunter with 500+ followers who can post on your behalf (higher visibility).

**3 days before:** Prepare all assets: 260×260px icon, demo GIF, tagline, first comment (your founder story).

**Launch day:** Post Tuesday–Thursday, 12:01am PST for maximum 24-hour window. Engage with EVERY comment. Share across all channels. Have supporters upvote (genuine supporters only — buying votes is against rules).

**Post-launch (+1 week):** Share results. "#1 Product of the Day" is a permanent badge and marketing asset.
